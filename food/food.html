Food Spots


New Zealand has got a lot of different options of where and what you can eat! Whereever should you want to go? Well we can help you figure out the dream meal you've been waiting for on your adventure in New Zealand!


Cafes


Havana Coffee Works

At 163 Tory Street in Wellington you can find Havana Coffee Works which is the perfect spot to have a nice afternoon coffee. Perfect for the coffee snobs!

Price range 10$-15$
Open 7.00am-5.00pm
Official Instagram
Official site

Catnap Cafe

391 Colombo Street, Sydenham, Christchurch is the adress of the most dreamy place you can ever think of!! Catnap Cafe offers the standard coffees and desserts. Though the real treasure is not the coffee and desserts, it is the cute and cuddly cats! The cats found in Catnap Cafe are rescues from Cat Rescue Christchurch, so that means you can adopt one of them and take them home with you! Not saying you have to, of course. They do allow walk-ins, but booking secures a guaranteed meet-up for you and the cats for 55 minutes and only for 16$!!!

Price range 16$
Open wednesday-sunday 10.00am-5.00pm
Official Instagram
Official site

Farmhouse Cafe

On 400 Papakura-Clevedon Road Clevedon, Auckland you can enjoy the most amazing sweets. With an interesting backstory, Farmhouse Cafe also provides delicious high tea and comes with the sight of seeing beautiful and unique animals. They offer a wide variety of nourishment and drinks! So are you wanting to feel nature in your heart through the experience of living in the moment and at the same time eating desserts that are out of this world? This is the perfect place for you.

Restaurants


Nest

At 139 Fernhill Road 9300 Queenstown you find yourselves at Kamana Lakehouse Hotel. Inside there, is where the Nest restaurant is located. Go here to enjoy a nice casual fine dining experience. Guests have described Nest as being filled with a nice and relaxed atmosphere. This is a famous place within the community for having the local ingredients in the dishes. Perfect for people who love to taste a diverse amount of cuisine. They have an easy to understand and pick from the menu, so come and enjoy a relaxed evening here.


Price range 100$-150$
Open 7.00am-10.00pm
Official Instagram
Official site

Big Fig Wanaka

105 Ardmore St, Wanaka is where Big Fig is at! Eating delicious meals is of course a good thing, but a more important aspect is eating healthy. Which is exactly why you should eat out at Big Fig! They offer a wide variety of healthy meals, so there's something for everyone!

Price range 40$-50$
Open 8.00am-8.30pm
Official Instagram
Official site

The Cow

At both Queenstown and Wanaka are the locations of The Cow. To be more precise; 33 Ardmore St, Wanaka 9305 and Cow Lane, Queenstown 9300. The Cow is an Italian restaurant which has pizza and spaghetti in house. This is one of the must-visit restaurants in Wanaka, for their pizza is just so amazing.

Price range 40$-60$
Open Queenstown; 12.00pm-11.00pm. Wanaka 5.00pm-9.30pm
