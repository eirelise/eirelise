Silver Fern

The Silver Fern is New Zealand's national plant! Though it is known as Pongo as the Maori name. This plant has been representing New Zealand ever since the 1880s!

Kiwi

The national bird of New Zealand is the Kiwi bird. Though it can not fly and has hairy like feathers, it still has its own beauty. The iconic Kiwi has got 5 races, and some are endangered like the Rowi.

This flightless bird with an age span of around 50 years is actually surprisingly speedy, its legs are really strong and it can run up to and more than 20km/h. Kiwi birds are omnivores, and just like many other birds, they eat berries, seeds, leaves and a variety of insects.

New Zealanders

Did you know that the inhabitants of New Zealand call themselves Kiwi? Ever since world war 1, new zealanders have been calling themselves kiwi, which is a symbol of pride and affection.
